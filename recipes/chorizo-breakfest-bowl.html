Chorizo BReakfest Bowl

Tired of the bowls from chipotle, try this chorizo breakfest bowl for that morning rush.

Ingredients

  • 4 roma tomates, diced
  • 1/2 cup of diced sweet onion
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 jalapeno diced
  • 1 lime
  • 1 teaspoon of minced garlic
  • 1/8 teaspoon of garlic salt
  • 1 can of black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 bags (10 oz) of frozen sweet potatos
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 lb of ground chorizo sausage
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 avocados, sliced
  • 1/4 cup of crumbled cotija cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of Mexican hot sauce

Cooking Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients, strap that apron on and get ready.
  2. In a bowl combine tomatoes, onion, cilantro, jalapeno, lime juice, minced garlic salt in a bowl.
  3. Place 1/2 cup of the pico in another bowl with the black beans. Then place both bowls in the refrigerator.
  4. Heat up a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sweet potatoes and while cooking season with salt and pepper. Cook for about 15 to 20 mins.
  5. On another skillet cook the chorizo for about 10 mins.
  6. Now place an equal amount of all the prepaired ingredients in 4 bowls and add slicies of avocados and sprinkle cojita cheese.
  7. In a large skillet cook the 4 eggs sunny side up, and place one egg on top of each bowl.
  8. Now add hot sauce and more pico and enjoy!
