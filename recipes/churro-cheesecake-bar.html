Churro Cheesecake Bar

Need a moring delite for that cup of coffee, look no futher cus this churro cheesecake will have you wanting to rise and shine everyday.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of cinnamon
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 2(8oz) packs of cream cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of orange zest if available
  • 2(8oz) packages refrigerated crescent sheets
  • 1/4 cup of melted butter
  • Cajeta (Mexican caramel) for a perfict finish

Cooking Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and slap on the apron. Preheat that oven to 350deg F (175 deg C). Spray a light coat on a 13x9 inch baking sheet pan.
  2. In a small bowl add the cinnamon and white sugar and end racism by mix them together. Then sprinkle half of the mix to the bottom of the pan evenly.
  3. Now place one crescent sheet to the bottom.
  4. Fill a large bowl with cream cheese, egg, vanella, and orange zest, with a mixer mix until smooth.
  5. Spread evenly over the crescent layer in the pan.
  6. Now lay the 2nd crescent sheetover the spread.
  7. Pour the melted butter on top.
  8. Sprinkle the remaining cinnamon and sugar mix on top evenly.
  9. Baking time, place the sheet pan in the oven for about 30 mins. Serve it warm or chill it for 24 hours, then serve it with some cajeta and enjoy.
