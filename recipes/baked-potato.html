Baked Potato

Baking and waking time, this baked potato recipe will make you want to start a rap beef with Kendrick Lamar.

On a real note, its a delicous meal for anyone, unless theirs a complete dislike to potatos.

Ingredients

  • 2 or more baking potatoes
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of slap yo mama
  • 1 pack of bacon
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 8 green onions

Cooking Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat oven to 350degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Place potatoes on a sheet tray and bake for about an hour, once done set aside for cooldown
  3. In the meantime place bacon on another sheet tray and bake for 13 mins, once done drain, crumble and set aside
  4. once potatoes have cooled down, slice them in half lengthwise and scoop the insides into a large bowl, save the skins for later
  5. In another bowl add sour cream, milk, salt, papper, 1/2 cup of cheese, 1/2 of the green onions; mix until creamy
  6. With a spoon add the the mixture to the skin shells from earlier adding the bacon and cheese on top
  7. Now to finish it off place the potatoes back in the oven for 10 mins and enjoy
